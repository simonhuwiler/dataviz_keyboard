A bright dot – the ball – and two bars – the paddle – Pong was a simple video game. It is not only the grandfather of video games, it was one of the games played at the first «eSport» tournament. In 1972, students organized the «Intergalactic spacewar Olympics». First prize: A one-year subscription for the «Rolling Stone» magazine.
2015 2016 2017 2018
Time has changed. From simple ping pong adaptations to strategic complex simulations. eSports, competitive sports using video games, is a thriving market. Around 130 Millions in 2012. Twenty years later: 1.9 billion dollars estimated!
But let's focus on the athletes. «The international» is a well-known e-sports tournament for Valves «Dota-2»-Game. The winning teams earns fame – and cash. Have a look at your keyboard. Lets say that each key on your keyboard has a value around 250’000 dollars. What you see is the price pool at the first tournament back in 2011.
And that was the price pool couple month ago. 34 Million dollars!
As always is sports: If you are at the top, you can make a living. Best payed player Johan Sundstein (Danmark), Hesse Vainikka (Finland) and Anathan Pham (Australia) made between six and seven million dollars in their career.

The measuring unit in eSports is actions per minute (apm). An action is selection a unit or giving orders to your meeple. In intense battle, the APM is around 400. Look at your keyboard. That’s how fast you would have to write.
